About Us


Suzie

Pianist, harpsichordist, and conductor Suzanne Cartreine founded Turkey Hill Ragtime Orchestra in 2022. Combining a lifetime love of ragtime with an academic interest in historical performance practice, she was intrigued to discover the repertoire of orchestral arrangements of well-known and little-known rags, and excited to hear and play them.

Suzie is the Music Director for Tufts University Chaplaincy, where she founded and directs an interfaith community choir, Voices for Peace and Light, made up of students, staff, faculty, alum, and community members. Additionally, she is the Director of Music at the Unitarian Universalist Society of Wellesley Hills.

Suzie has a DMus in Historical Performance from Boston University, a MMus in Piano Performance from Temple University (Pi Kappa Lambda), and a BA in Physics from Macalester College (Phi Beta Kappa, summa cum laude). When not making music, Suzie enjoys yoga, swimming, playing cards, and spending time with her husband and two sons.

About Us

Toe-tapping fun for the whole family - the 10-13 piece Turkey Hill Ragtime Orchestra plays vintage theater orchestra arrangements of ragtime music and popular songs from the 1890's - 1920's. Fox-trots, slow drags, two-steps, waltzes, and more! Featuring works of Joplin, Salisbury, Lamb, Turpin, Berlin, and others.
